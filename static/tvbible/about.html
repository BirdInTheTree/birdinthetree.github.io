About tvbible

tvbible is a free knowledge base for pitching TV series — synthesized from six books on the craft of the pitch bible: Nash, Harris, Hauge, Landau, Oberg, Levy.

Each card is one atomic idea: a principle, a pitfall, a criterion, or a show example. Cards link to each other using [[wikilinks]], forming a navigable graph of knowledge.
