Библия сериала (series bible)

Питч
oberg

Суть

Библия (она же format, book, TV treatment, pitch document или proposal) — документ, содержащий информацию о персонажах, сеттинге, темах, тоне, стиле, структуре и сюжетных линиях шоу [1]. Она служит трём целям: как инструмент продажи для питча, как творческий компас для разработки и как справочник при продакшене [2]. Библия обязательна и при пилотном пути, и при straight-to-series — даже если пилот написан первым, для питча серии нужна библия [3].

Цитаты

[1] "A bible (also called format, book, TV treatment, pitch document or proposal) is a document that contains information on the characters, settings, themes, tone, style, structure, and storylines of a show. It is used to pitch a series idea to producers, networks, or studios, as well as to guide the writers and showrunners during the production process." — Oberg, Chapter 3, Section 3.1, p.166

[2] "The bible serves three main purposes: 1) As a selling tool — to pitch the series idea and get it commissioned. 2) As a creative compass — to guide development and keep everyone aligned. 3) As a reference document — to keep track of key information during production." — Oberg, Chapter 3, Section 3.1, p.167

[3] "Whether you're going the pilot route or the straight-to-series route, having a pitch bible is essential. Even if you write the pilot first, you'll still need a bible to pitch the series." — Oberg, Chapter 3, Section 3.1, p.168
