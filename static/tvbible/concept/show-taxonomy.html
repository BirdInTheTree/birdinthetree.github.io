Типы шоу и классификация франшиз

Мир сериала
obergnashlevy

Суть

Существует несколько основных типов сериалов: procedural (case-of-the-week), serial (open-ended), hybrid, sitcom, anthology и limited series [1]. Nash выделяет семь franchise-моделей: Trapped Together, Blank of the Week, (Wo)Man with a Plan, Dude with a Season-Long Problem, WhyDunIt, I'm No Fool, Buddy Love и Mad Mad World [2][3][4]. Levy классифицирует четыре типа: case-of-the-week, soap, power и family [5]. Большинство успешных сериалов -- это blend нескольких типов франшизы [6][7].

Цитаты

[1] "There are five main series types today: Procedural (closed-ended), Serial (open-ended), Serial-Procedural Hybrid, Sitcom and Mini-Series (Limited Series). There is also the Anthology Series which brings a new world and set of characters in each episode." -- Oberg, Chapter 1, Section 1.2, p.46

[2] "Trapped Together -- A group of characters are stuck in a shared location or situation. The comedy or drama comes from their forced proximity." -- Nash, p.40

[3] "Blank of the Week -- Each episode brings a new case, patient, monster, or problem. The franchise is the pipeline of new weekly challenges." -- Nash, p.41

[4] "WhyDunIt -- The mystery drives the show. Who did it? Why? How? The franchise is the question." -- Nash, p.44

[5] "The four most common franchise types in TV drama are case-of-the-week, soap, power and family." -- Levy, p.56

[6] "Most shows are a blend of franchise types. But there's usually one dominant type." -- Nash, p.48

[7] "Most successful series blend franchise types. Breaking Bad combined a power franchise (Walter White's rise in the drug world) with a family franchise (the effect of his secret on his family)." -- Levy, p.65
