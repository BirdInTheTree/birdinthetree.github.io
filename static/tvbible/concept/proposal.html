Серийное предложение (proposal)

Питч
harrislevyoberg

Суть

Series proposal — документ из 3–6 страниц, который начинается с one-sheet как вводной страницы, затем раскрывает каждый элемент подробнее [1]. Overview (он же one-sheet или series overview) — 1–2 страницы, расширяющие логлайн: мир, тон, главные герои, посылка, уникальность проекта [2]. Для straight-to-series проектов Levy описывает расширенную библию: видение сериала, арки нескольких сезонов и поэпизодные синопсисы первого-второго сезонов [3]. Oberg подчёркивает, что даже при пилотном пути нужна библия для питча серии в целом [4].

Цитаты

[1] "If you are pitching a series, you will almost certainly be asked for a written series proposal. This is a longer document of three to six pages which begins with your summary one-sheet as an introductory page, and then takes each element and expands on it." — Harris, p.153

[2] "The overview (also known as one-sheet or series overview) is a one to two page document that expands on the logline. It gives a broader picture of the series: the world, the tone, the main characters, the premise and what makes the series unique." — Oberg, Chapter 3, Bible Components, p.198

[3] "Straight-to-series projects usually incorporate an extensive 'series bible,' a prose document that states the vision of the series and its creative direction for the life of the series, an outline of story arcs of the first several seasons, and detailed summaries of every story of every episode of the first season or two." — Levy, p.242

[4] "Whether you're going the pilot route or the straight-to-series route, having a pitch bible is essential. Even if you write the pilot first, you'll still need a bible to pitch the series." — Oberg, Chapter 3, Section 3.1, p.168
