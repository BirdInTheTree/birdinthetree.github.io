Showrunning

levylandau

Суть

Creator — автор, изначально придумавший идею сериала и написавший пилотный сценарий; считается самым важным человеком в разработке [1]. Showrunner — writer/producer, управляющий сериалом на ежедневной основе: руководит штатом сценаристов, контролирует производство и пост-продакшн, поддерживает creative vision [2]. В кино главный — режиссёр; в ТВ — showrunner [3]. Non-writing EP — опытный development-профессионал, помогающий авторам разрабатывать проекты, а showrunner'ам — с продюсерской стороной работы [4].

Цитаты

[1] "The creator is the writer who originally conceived the idea for the series and wrote the pilot script. As such, the creator is the originating artist and is generally considered the most important person in the development of any TV series." — Levy, p.14

[2] "The showrunner is the writer/producer who manages the series on a day-to-day basis once it's ordered to series and goes into production. Showrunners manage the writing staff and the scripts they produce, they oversee series production and post-production, and they maintain the creative vision of the series." — Levy, p.15

[3] "In movies, the director is king. In the television series business, the showrunner calls the shots. A showrunner is almost always the head writer and executive producer of a TV series." — Landau, p.19

[4] "Non-writing EPs are highly experienced development professionals who partner with writers to help develop their projects and, once a show gets on the air, to assist showrunners with the producing side of the job." — Levy, p.17
