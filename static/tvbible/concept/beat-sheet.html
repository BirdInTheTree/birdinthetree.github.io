Beat sheet эпизода

Структура
harrisnash

Суть

Beat sheet — это «машина трансформации», последовательность из 15 битов, которая проводит героя от изъяна к изменению (или отказу от изменения) [1]. Ключевые биты работают парами «действие/реакция»: Catalyst порождает Debate, Break into 2 — Fun and Games, Midpoint — Bad Guys Close In [2]. Пять опорных (pillar) битов — Catalyst, Midpoint, All Is Lost, Break into 3, Finale — составляют скелет, вокруг которого достраиваются остальные [3]. При этом beat sheet — это маршрут, а не расписание: биты могут расширяться, сжиматься и перемещаться [4]. Каждая история, от сцены до сезона, содержит одно и то же Story DNA: герой, цель, препятствие, ставки [5].

Цитаты

[1] "The Save the Cat! beat sheet is a transformation machine." — Nash, p.13

[2] "Beats come in action/reaction pairs. The Catalyst is the action; the Debate is the reaction. Break into 2 is the action; Fun and Games is the reaction. Midpoint is the action; Bad Guys Close In is the reaction." — Nash, p.96

[3] "The five pillar beats: Catalyst, Midpoint, All Is Lost, Break into 3, and Finale." — Nash, p.94

[4] "The TV beat sheet is an itinerary, not a calendar of appointments. It's flexible. Beats can expand, contract, move around." — Nash, p.100

[5] "Story DNA has four parts: Hero, Goal, Obstacle, Stakes." — Nash, p.34

[6] "Opening Image — A snapshot of the hero and their world. This image will be mirrored in the Final Image to show how much has changed." — Nash, p.14

[7] "Catalyst — Something happens. The doorbell rings. The fax arrives. A body is found. This event is the thing that gets the story going." — Nash, p.14

[8] "The Catalyst is the single most important beat. It's the thing that happens that makes everything else happen." — Nash, p.17

[9] "Break into 2 is a decision, not an event. Something happens to the hero (Catalyst), they react (Debate), and then they CHOOSE to enter the new world." — Nash, p.18

[10] "Fun and Games — The promise of the premise. This is why we bought the ticket. It's the fun part." — Nash, p.15

[11] "Midpoint — A false peak or a false collapse. Stakes are raised. A ticking clock appears. The fun is over. Things are about to get real." — Nash, p.15

[12] "The Midpoint raises the stakes. What was personal becomes public. What was fun becomes serious. The Midpoint is where the game changes." — Nash, p.18

[13] "All Is Lost — The lowest point. A whiff of death. Something dies here, literally or metaphorically." — Nash, p.15

[14] "The Dark Night of the Soul is where the hero faces their flaw head-on. They can't run from it anymore. They have to decide who they're going to be." — Nash, p.19

[15] "Break into 3 — The hero discovers the solution. Often, the B Story character has helped the hero understand the theme." — Nash, p.15

[16] "The Five Point Finale: 1) Gathering the Team — the hero assembles what they need. 2) Executing the Plan — they go for it. 3) High Tower Surprise — it doesn't work. 4) Dig Deep Down — the hero uses what they've learned. 5) Execution of the New Plan — they succeed (or fail) in a new way." — Nash, p.20

[17] "Synthesis is the key to the Finale. The hero combines what they knew at the beginning with what they learned along the way to create something new." — Nash, p.20

[18] "The Set-Up is where you plant everything. Every gun on the wall in the Set-Up must fire by the Finale. Every payoff in the Finale must be planted in the Set-Up." — Nash, p.17

[19] "Some beats are single scenes. Some beats are sequences. Some beats are entire acts. The beat sheet doesn't care about length. It cares about function." — Nash, p.100

[20] "Five steps for pillar beats: 1) Start with the Catalyst. 2) Jump to All Is Lost. 3) Figure out the Midpoint between them. 4) Find the Break into 3. 5) Build the Finale." — Nash, p.148

[21] "Before you can pitch a script, you need to understand the essential building blocks of a good story - the blocks that your pitch will be built on." — Harris, p.12

Связи

  • Актовая структура эпизода — beat sheet наполняет актовую структуру конкретным содержанием
  • Пилотный эпизод — пилот использует beat sheet с модификациями (Break into Series вместо Break into 3)
  • Конструкция сцены — каждый бит может быть одной сценой или последовательностью сцен
  • Cold open (тизер) — Opening Image / teaser — первый бит, задающий тон
