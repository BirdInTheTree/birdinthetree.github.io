Every screenwriting book has a pitch bible template. Four sections: Hook, World, Characters, Story. Neat and balanced. Then you open the real documents. The Wire devoted 76% of its pages to Story. Stranger Things gave 65% to World. Desperate Housewives was 71% Characters. Same four ingredients — wildly different recipes. These visualizations dissect 11 real bibles that actually sold.
